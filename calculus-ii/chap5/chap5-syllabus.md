# Chapter 5: Fourier Series

**Syllabus Focus: Illustrate periodic functions of practical importance by infinite trigonometric series. (5 hours)**

* 5.1 Periodic Functions, odd and even functions
* 5.2 Fourier series of $2\pi$ periodic functions in the interval $(\alpha, \alpha + 2\pi)$.
* 5.3 Fourier series of $2l$ periodic functions.
* 5.4 Fourier series of odd and even functions, sine and cosine series

**List of Tutorials (4 hours total):**
* 5.1 Problems on Fourier series in $(-\pi, \pi), (0,2\pi)$ and $(-\frac{\pi}{2}, \frac{3\pi}{2})$. (2 hrs)
* 5.2 Problems on Fourier series of odd and even functions, Fourier series in general interval $(-l, l)$. (2 hrs)
